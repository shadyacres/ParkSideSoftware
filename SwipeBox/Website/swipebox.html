Swipe Box is a product that aims to give a customer a more personal welcome to an office building. When the customer enters the building they will swipe their assigned NFC card on the Swipe Box which will then send a message to the receptionist with personalized information like their name, what company they are from and the purpose of their most recent visit.

Swipe Box uses the Raspberry Pi B+ which allows for more functionality in the future including QR code scanning.
